The Boost

Holder of Mini Miners can choose to “boost” their miner to earn more rune tokens. There is a 50% chance that the miner will be burned if boosted. All holders of our original collection, Ordinal Minis, will be granted immunity during the boost.

Tokenomics

In true Bitcoin fashion, the supply of the runes will be 21b tokens.

The token distribution goes as follows:

- 72% to base miners - 23% to boosted miners 5% for the team

All tokens will follow the runes standard by @rodarmor.
